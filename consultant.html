Cultural Verification Guidelines

Historical Accuracy

  • Verify traditional crafting techniques are accurately described
  • Ensure cultural significance is properly explained
  • Check for respectful representation of tribal heritage

Artistic Authenticity

  • Confirm use of traditional materials and methods
  • Validate symbolic meanings and patterns
  • Ensure proper attribution to specific tribes/regions

Cultural Sensitivity

  • Check for appropriate use of sacred symbols
  • Ensure respectful language in descriptions
  • Verify no cultural appropriation concerns

Verification Process

  • Review product description and cultural significance
  • Examine product images for authenticity
  • Provide detailed feedback for improvements
  • Approve only when all criteria are met
